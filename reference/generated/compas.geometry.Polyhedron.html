Polyhedron

class compas.geometry.Polyhedron[source]

Bases: object

Compute the vertices and faces of one of the Platonic solids.

Notes

A Platonic solid is a regular, convex polyhedron. It is constructed by congruent regular polygonal faces with the same number of faces meeting at each vertex 1.
