mesh_from_surface_uv

compas_rhino.helpers.mesh_from_surface_uv(cls, guid, density=(10, 10))[source]

Construct a mesh from a point cloud aligned with the uv space of a Rhino NURBS surface.

Parameters

  • cls (Mesh) – The mesh type.

  • guid (str) – The GUID of the surface.

  • density (tuple) – The density of the point grid in the u and v directions.
